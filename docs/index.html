HausaNLP

HausaNLP is a research group for Hausa Natural Language Processing. The group is part of BUKNLP research group and other researchers with interest in HausaNLP. If you are a student or researcher and wants to join us to advance research in Hausa natural language processing, we are happy to collaborate.
